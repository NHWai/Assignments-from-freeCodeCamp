Vintage Fashion

The trend for "vintage" clothing as fashion exploded in the 1960s. Prior to this, the trading and wearing of old clothing had different connotations. As the commercial constituency for fashion increased, the growth in trade of old clothing was incremental as the quantity of these goods increased.
